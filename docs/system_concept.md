# System Concept

## Mission

The case study system is an **Autonomous Wildfire Detection Drone System** intended to survey a forest sector, detect thermal anomalies, and alert operators quickly enough to support early response.

## Operational View

- The drone is launched with a mission profile from the ground control station.
- The payload captures thermal imagery and generates hotspot candidates.
- The mission computer fuses payload output with position state.
- The communications subsystem forwards alerts and telemetry to the ground station.
- The ground station records operator actions and mission events for later review.

## Primary Subsystems

- airframe and autopilot
- thermal payload
- mission computer
- communications link
- ground control station
- power subsystem
